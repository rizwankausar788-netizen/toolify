Frequently Asked Questions

When does DEPTHXPO launch? +
The closed developer beta launches in Q3 2026, with a public deployment release shortly following.
What phones are supported? +
Any modern Android phone running Android 10 or above containing a working accelerometer/gyroscope hardware matrix sensor array.
Is it safe for mobile battery life? +
Yes. Because the engine runs natively on low-level Flutter bindings, animation tracking sleeps completely when the device screen is off or another app covers the window.
